# Join Google Meet - User Guide

## For Therapists 👨‍⚕️

### Overview
When patients book sessions with you, Google Meet links are automatically generated. You can now join these sessions directly from your dashboard with a single click.

---

### Step-by-Step: Joining a Session

#### 1. Log In to Dashboard
- Go to: `http://localhost:5175/therapist/dashboard`
- Enter your therapist credentials

#### 2. View Booked Sessions
- The right column shows **"Booked Sessions"** with count
- Each session card shows:
  - **Patient Name**
  - **Date**: "Wed, Apr 19"
  - **Time**: "14:00"
  - **Countdown**: "Starts in 2h 30m"

#### 3. Join the Session
- Click the **blue "Join Google Meet"** button
- Browser opens the meeting in a new tab
- You're now in the Google Meet session!

## For Patients 👨‍💼

### Overview
When you book a session with a therapist, a Google Meet link is generated automatically. You can join the session from your dashboard.

---

### Step-by-Step: Joining a Session

#### 1. Log In to Dashboard
- Go to: `http://localhost:5175/user/dashboard`
- Enter your patient credentials

#### 2. View Your Sessions
- Click **"Dashboard"** tab
- Look for upcoming sessions

#### 3. Join When Ready
- When session time approaches, click **"Join Session"**
- Browser opens the meeting
- You're in the Google Meet!

---

## Troubleshooting

### Can't See "Join Session" Button?

**Reasons**:
- Session hasn't been booked yet
- Session time has passed
- Google Meet link not generated
- Browser cache issue

**Solutions**:
1. Refresh page (F5)
2. Clear browser cache (Ctrl+Shift+Delete)
3. Log out and log back in
4. Try different browser
5. Contact support

---

### Meeting Link Opens Blank Page?

**Reasons**:
- Internet connection issue
- Google Meet servers down
- Browser popup blocker active

**Solutions**:
1. Check internet connection
2. Wait 30 seconds and refresh
3. Disable browser popup blocker
4. Try different browser
5. Try mobile app instead

---

### Audio/Video Not Working?

**Solutions**:
1. Check browser permissions (Settings > Site Settings)
2. Test device audio in system settings
3. Try different browser
4. Restart computer
5. Use phone instead

---

### Can't Find Your Session?

**Check**:
- [ ] Correct date and time?
- [ ] Session confirmed/booked?
- [ ] Not already completed?
- [ ] Time zone correct?
- [ ] Page fully loaded?

## FAQ

**Q: Can I join from my phone?**
A: Yes! Google Meet works on mobile. Click the link and it opens in your browser or Meet app.

**Q: What if I'm late?**
A: Click the button whenever you're ready. The therapist may wait up to 15 minutes.

**Q: Can I save the link for later?**
A: Each session has its own unique link. It's created fresh for each booking.

**Q: Is the session recorded?**
A: Only if the therapist explicitly enables recording. You'll be notified.

**Q: How long does the session last?**
A: Standard sessions are 1 hour. Check your booking confirmation for exact duration.

**Q: Can I leave and rejoin?**
A: Yes, but the therapist won't be notified. Let them know if you need to step away.

**Q: What if Google Meet crashes?**
A: Both users will be disconnected. Try rejoin using the same link.
